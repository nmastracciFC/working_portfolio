HOW TO PLAY

How to Play

DOWNLOAD APP

The app is available at all app stores. Once you download the app you will be prompted to create a profile of your character. Choose your everything from your hair colour to general disposition!

CHOOSE COMPANION

Every good adventurer has a side kick--someone to help point out his mistakes and that it's his own fault it he gets lost. For your adventure you may choose either Gollum or Sam to accompany you. Choose Wisely.

COLLECT RINGS

While walking around with the app open you will be notified if you come upon something to collect. Collect jewels to use as currency, potions to help defeat attackers and RINGS to win the game.

CHALLENGE FRIENDS

As if you needed an excuse to be competitive with them... the LOTR Adventure App lets you duke it out with your buddies in real time! Simply make sure to be within 15ft of each other(BUT NO CLOSER) for your attacks to land. Let the potion throwing begin!
